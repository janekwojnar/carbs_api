# Product Requirements (V1 -> Elite)

## Vision
Create the best-in-class carb/fueling intelligence platform for endurance and hybrid athletes, with explainable AI and practical race/training execution.

## Core users
- Athletes (primary UX first)
- Coaches
- Dietitians

## Supported sports (V1)
- Running
- Cycling
- Swimming
- Hiking
- Trail Running
- Gym
- HIIT
- Hyrox

## Mandatory capabilities
- Carbs targets (g/h)
- Pre/during/post fueling plans
- Hydration and sodium recommendations
- GI risk scoring
- Periodized carb strategy calendar
- Race strategy planner
- Live adjustment suggestions
- Explainable AI output
- Strategy variants: aggressive/balanced/conservative
- Confidence intervals and uncertainty communication

## Inputs
- Physiology: body mass, body fat, VO2max, lactate threshold
- Training load/history
- Nutrition habits and GI tolerance
- Menstrual context
- CGM (optional)
- Sleep/HRV
- Stress/illness/injury flags
- Environment: heat, humidity, altitude, terrain
- Session context: indoor/outdoor, duration, intensity, sport type

## Integrations (V1 priority)
- Garmin
- Strava
- TrainingPeaks
- Zwift
- Apple Health
- CSV import/export
- Webhooks

## Constraints
- Fitness/wellness classification
- EU/Poland launch in English
- Uptime target: 99.9%
- Full audit logs
- Formal validation study before broad scale

## KPI targets (12 months)
- 10,000 paying subscribers
- 30-day retention > 45%
- Plan adherence > 60%
- Prediction satisfaction score > 4.5/5
